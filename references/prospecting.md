# Prospecting & Account Planning Reference

## ICP Dimensions

**Firmographic:** Revenue/ARR range, employee count, industry vertical, geography, growth stage
**Technographic:** Current tech stack, existing contract expiry, build-vs-buy signal
**Behavioral:** Hiring for roles that indicate the problem you solve, publishing content on the pain you address

**Account tiers:**
- Tier 1 (Strategic): Top 20 accounts — full account plan, 1:1 outreach, executive alignment
- Tier 2 (Target): Next 50 — sequenced outreach, personalized at company level
- Tier 3 (Scalable): Rest of territory — templated with light personalization

---

## Trigger Events

| Trigger | Opening |
|---------|---------|
| New executive hire (C-suite) | "Congrats to [Name] on joining — I'd love to share how we've helped similar leaders in their first 90 days" |
| Funding round | "Congrats on the Series C — you're in growth mode; here's how we help companies at your stage" |
| M&A / acquisition | "We've helped [X companies] navigate post-merger consolidation in [your area]" |
| New regulation | "With [Regulation] taking effect in [month], here's how we're helping [Industry] companies prepare" |
| Headcount growth | "I noticed you're hiring 10 [roles] — that usually means [problem you solve] is about to get harder" |

## Outbound Sequence (Tier 1: 8–10 touches over 3–4 weeks)

```
Day 1:  Email (trigger-based, personalized)
Day 3:  LinkedIn connection request
Day 5:  Email (different angle, add social proof)
Day 7:  Phone + voicemail
Day 10: Email (insight/content — give value with no ask)
Day 14: LinkedIn InMail or message
Day 17: Phone (reference prior outreach)
Day 21: Break-up email ("last try")
```

## Email Templates

### Trigger-based opener
```
Subject: [Specific initiative] at [Company]

[First name],

Congrats on joining [Company] as [Title] — exciting time given [specific thing].

I work with [Role] leaders at companies like [relevant peer] dealing with [specific problem].
[Company X] recently [result: reduced X by Y% / saved $Zm].

Given what [Company] is building, I suspect [specific hypothesis] might be on your radar.

Worth a 20-minute conversation?

[Name]
```

### Break-up email
```
Subject: Closing the loop

[First name],

I've reached out a few times — totally understand, you're busy.

I'll stop following up after this. But if [specific pain] ever becomes a priority,
I'd love to be the first call you make.

[Name]
```

## Hypothesis of Value

Before first contact, build a point of view that:
1. Demonstrates you've done your homework
2. Surfaces a problem they may not have fully quantified
3. Positions you as a strategic advisor, not a vendor

**Formula:**
> "[Company] is doing [X initiative/growth]. In our experience, companies at this stage typically face [specific challenge]. We've seen this cost [dollar amount / time / risk]. Here's what [Peer Company] did about it."

---

## Account Mapping

For Tier 1 accounts, map before outreach:
- **Economic Buyer (EB):** Who controls budget? (Usually VP+ or C-suite)
- **Champion candidate:** Who has the problem and motivation to fix it?
- **Influencers:** IT, Legal, Finance, end users
- **Blockers:** Who benefits from the status quo?

**Multi-threading rule:** Never rely on one contact. Aim for 3+ contacts at different levels before Stage 3.
